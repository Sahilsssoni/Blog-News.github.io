During the second Test between Sri Lanka and Australia, David Warner uses his stump microphone to send a humorous message to the announcer.
The second day of the second Test match, which is being played in Galle, included a really humorous event that was captured by the broadcasters of the Sri Lanka vs. Australia Test series. David Warner utilised the stump mic, one of the greatest new developments in the game from the perspective of umpiring reviews and for the enjoyment of fans, for an entirely other reason: to communicate with the commentary team.
